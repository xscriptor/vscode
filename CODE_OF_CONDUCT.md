<h1>Code of Conduct</h1>

<p>
Xscriptor and all contributors are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.
</p>

<h2>Our Standards</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Use respectful and inclusive language.</li>
  <li>Accept constructive feedback gracefully.</li>
  <li>Focus on what is best for the community and project.</li>
  <li>Show empathy toward other contributors and users.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Unacceptable Behavior</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.</li>
  <li>Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.</li>
  <li>Publishing private information without permission.</li>
  <li>Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Scope</h2>
<p>
This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, issue trackers, pull requests, and other community channels related to this repository.
</p>

<h2>Reporting</h2>
<p>
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at
<a href="mailto:x@xscriptor.com">x@xscriptor.com</a>.
</p>

<h2>Enforcement</h2>
<p>
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
</p>

<p>
By participating in this project, you agree to follow these standards.
</p>
